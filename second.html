Adidas EQT

Adidas Originals has had a strong year of releases when it comes to their EQT line. Adidas will release another variation of their latest adidas EQT Cushion ADV Mid model for the upcoming Spring season. The brand has designed two brand new colorways of this model.

Adidas EQT Cushion ADV

Core Black/Tri-Color

White/EQT Yellow-Silver Metallic

Dark Grey

For those that are interested in purchasing one of both of these adidas EQT Cushion ADV colorways, they will release on December 6th at select adidas Originals retailers. Retail price is set at $130.
