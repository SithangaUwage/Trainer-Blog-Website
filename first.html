Nike x Supreme

The Nike Air More Uptempo in collaboration with Supreme will feature the large SUP on the side, that also uses R on the heel and EME.Three colorways of the Supreme Nike Air More Uptempo has been spotted. Dressed in either Triple Black, Red and White or Gold and White.

Supreme x Nike Air More Uptempo

Metallic Gold/White

Black/Black-White

Varsity Red/White

The Supreme x Nike Air More Uptempo Suptempo is scheduled to release online at SupremeNewYork.com on April 27th, 2017 in the U.S. and Europe. Following they will debut in Japan in-store and online on April 29th. Retail price is set at $190 each.
